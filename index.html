Fusiomica sp. z o.o. is a biotechnology company specializing in the utilisation of protein engineering in drug discovery. Founded by experienced pharma/biotech managers with strong Cambridge links and based in Kraków at the Małopolska Centrum Biotechnologii, we aim to bridge the Polish and Cambridge biotech environments.

Our aim is to tackle the most challenging problems in drug discovery, leveraging novel technologies and prior experience from the most distinguished academic and commercial institutions.
