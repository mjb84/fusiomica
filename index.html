Fusiomica sp. z o.o. is a biotechnology company specializing in protein engineering and drug discovery. Based in Kraków at the Małopolska Centrum Biotechnologii, we aim to bridge the Polish and Cambridge biotech environments.

Founded by scientists who met in Cambridge, UK, our company integrates international expertise to tackle the most challenging drug discovery problems.

Our Mission

At Fusiomica, we are committed to advancing biotechnology through innovative solutions in protein engineering. Our team collaborates globally to develop cutting-edge approaches that accelerate drug discovery and deliver value to patients worldwide.
